Overview

Purpose

This is a website to promote traffic to the resort for people wishing to take a magical vacation away from home at an all inclusive spa/resort. We want to monitor the audience that is most likely to purchase tickets and create an informational platform for people traveling to our resort for the first time.

Audience

The audience I would be advertising to would be married couples, honeymooners, or singles in have the flexibility to travel whenever they would like. Peaceful colors like blues and peaches and creams will make it feel more relaxing. The Content will be whimsical and the fonts should be bold and elegant. The audience should be most likely in the middle class and up lifestyle, as the resort is on the higher end. The common access point for the website will be through the laptop/desktop. Content

Home page

Welcome to the Chamber Hill Resorts and Spa. This is the getaway that you have been dreaming of. Take the time to find inner peace as well as explore the beautiful islands of the Maldives. With beautiful waters, rich culture, and a lovely atmosphere, there is happiness to be found everywhere! Outside being all-inclusive, we offer tours of the island as well as fun activities to do during your stay. Experience fine dining, and many of the island's scenery, and enjoy very fun excursions here at Chamber Hill. We offer a a 24-hour spa that leaves our members rejuvenated, and ready to take on the world. Our goal is to make sure that you come and leave with a little bit of the sunshine with you. Our Resort and Spa is fueled by the calming salt waters and the natural resources our island has to offer. Our staff is also trained in a variety of skills that are meant to heal the mind, body, and soul. Take your first steps now, and make a reservation to paradise.

About Us

25 Years strong, our resort has had the pleasure of hosting all who've come to relax and enjoy the beauty of the Maldives, Started by a small family business back in 1988, Chamber Hill grew from a small bed and breakfast to now being one of the most sought after couples retreat in the Indian Oceans. Our resort started as an escape for newlyweds who wanted some peaceful time after the hectic and stressful wedding planning. This was where we decided that not only our members deserved peace, but they also deserved to be pampered for all their hard work. We know that it takes a village to accomplish all the things you have accomplished, and it is now time for you to get the individual rest that you long deserved.
